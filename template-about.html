WHO AM I?

HI, I'M TERENCE

I am an aspiring Prototype Designer and Web Developer with comprehensive knowledge of design thinking. Through implementing, testing, and revising, I am skilled in creating systems with a clear vision, and one that offers great user experience.

It was during my high school career that I found my passion for creating, and this desire led me to study at the School of Interactive Arts & Technology, which taught me that designing is not a linear process. Since then, I have worked individually and in teams, to practice with applying feedback into creating new iterations.
